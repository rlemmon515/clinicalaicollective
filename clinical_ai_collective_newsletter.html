What the Clinical AI Brief Newsletter is not

No hype. No generic tips.
No AI cheerleading.

The Clinical AI Brief Newsletter is grounded in the same commitments as the platform: AI assists, clinicians decide, and every recommendation reflects the professional ethics and scope of practice that govern your license.

The Clinical AI Brief Newsletter will not tell you AI is going to transform healthcare and leave you to figure out what that means for your daily notes. It will tell you specifically what to do, why it is ethical to do it, and how to review it before it becomes part of the clinical record.
