The cost of doing nothing

Documentation is not just annoying.

Allied health clinicians spend between 35 and 40 percent of their workday on documentation. For a full-time clinician seeing eight patients a day, that is two to three hours every single day on notes instead of on care.

AI will not eliminate documentation. But even a 30 percent reduction in documentation time returns hours to your day — and your career — that compound over years. The clinicians getting ahead of this now are the ones who will have time, energy, and clinical judgment left at the end of the decade.

Every week of AI CEA prepares you for the next. By the end of Week 4 you will have a complete, deployable clinical AI tool built around your specific credential and documentation tasks — ready to use the following Monday.

Week 1

The AI Assistant Mindset

Reframe AI as a non-clinical productivity tool — not a replacement for your judgment. Establish your ethical foundation, understand how AI language models actually work, and write your first structured clinical prompt using a four-component framework built for allied health documentation.

Deliverable: Your first structured clinical prompt and a completed output review
Week 2

Structured Data and Triage

Transform raw clinical shorthand into formatted, discipline-specific documentation. Learn the three-category triage framework that separates your clinical observations from impressions before anything touches an AI tool — and leave with a personal ethics checklist you run on every output.

Deliverable: Three structured prompts from real-world clinical note scenarios + your ethics checklist
Week 3

Quality Control and Clinical Voice

Audit AI-generated documentation for hallucinations, factual errors, and scope violations. Build your custom instruction set and clinical voice profile so every AI output sounds exactly like you — not a generic template. Know what ASHA, AOTA, and APTA say, and what it means in practice.

Deliverable: Audited documentation samples, custom instruction set, and clinical voice profile
Week 4

Scaling the Workflow

The capstone build. Design and deploy a complete prompt library or clinical AI configuration targeting your most time-consuming weekly documentation task. Peer showcase, live revision, and a video walkthrough you can share with your employer or colleagues.

Deliverable: A complete, deployable clinical AI tool with user guide and video walkthrough
